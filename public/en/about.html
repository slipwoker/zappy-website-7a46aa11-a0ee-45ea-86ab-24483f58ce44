    Our Journey to Transform Urban Balconies into Designed Retreat Spaces

    The Story of Urban Balcony

    Urban Balcony was born from a deep love of nature and a desire to bring its beauty into modern urban life. We understood that residents of the central area, living in apartments and high-rise buildings, yearn for their own green corner - a place where they can relax, disconnect from routine, and connect with nature even in the heart of the bustling city.

    Our journey began when we discovered that small balconies and urban rooftops are an untapped resource that can become amazing spaces of beauty and tranquility. We decided to specialize in rooftop and balcony garden design, turning each project into a unique story of transformation and creativity. Today, we are proud to be the leaders in urban gardening in central Israel.

    Over the years we have developed unique expertise in dealing with specific challenges of urban balconies - space limitations, varying wind conditions and sun exposure, and minimal maintenance requirements. We combine deep botanical knowledge with advanced design sense, creating solutions that are both beautiful and practical.

    Our Mission

    To transform every urban balcony into a dream space that reflects the personality and style of its owners. We believe that every person deserves their own green space, and we work to make this vision a reality through professional design, personal service, and uncompromising commitment to quality.

    Our Vision

    To be the leading company in rooftop and urban balcony garden design in Israel, and to leave a mark of beauty and sustainability in every project. We aspire to create an urban gardening movement that enriches the quality of life for city residents and contributes to biodiversity and environmental well-being.

    Our Professional Approach to Rooftop Garden Design

    At Urban Balcony we believe that every project is unique and requires a personally tailored approach. Our work process begins with truly listening to your needs, dreams and lifestyle. We don't offer generic or template solutions - every design is created especially for you.

    We combine deep botanical knowledge with understanding of urban gardening challenges. We know which plants will thrive in the specific exposure conditions of your balcony, how to solve drainage problems, and how to create a space that will remain beautiful and functional over time. Our approach combines careful planning with precise execution and attention to every small detail.

    We also believe in education and knowledge transfer. At the completion of each project, we provide comprehensive guidance for ongoing maintenance, including tips for watering, pruning, and fertilizing. Our goal is not only to create a beautiful garden, but also to empower you to maintain it and enjoy it over time.

    Our Commitment to Sustainability

    At Urban Balcony we believe that urban gardening should be responsible and sustainable. We work to minimize the environmental impact of our projects and encourage urban biodiversity. This includes using native plants that require less water and care, smart irrigation systems that conserve water, and organic fertilizers that don't pollute the environment.

    We also encourage our clients to adopt environmentally friendly gardening practices, such as composting, rainwater collection, and creating habitats for butterflies and bees. We believe that every urban balcony can contribute to the urban ecosystem and improve air quality and city life.
