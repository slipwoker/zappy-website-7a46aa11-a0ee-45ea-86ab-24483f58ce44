    Welcome to Urban Balcony

    We at Urban Balcony believe that every urban balcony can become a personal sanctuary of tranquility and beauty. Our expertise in rooftop garden and small balcony design enables us to create unique solutions specially tailored for residents of the central region. We combine modern urban aesthetics with practical functionality, creating spaces that invite you to relax and connect with nature even in the heart of the city.

    Our professional team specializes in planning and designing rooftop gardens suitable for the Israeli climate and urban lifestyle. We understand the unique challenges of small balconies and rooftops, and offer creative solutions that maximize every centimeter while maintaining an elegant and inviting appearance.

    Our leading rooftop garden design services

    Comprehensive planning and design

    We offer comprehensive planning and design services that begin with understanding your needs and vision. Our team creates detailed plans including selection of suitable plants, smart irrigation solutions, and design of comfortable seating areas. Each project is customized to your budget and personal style.

    Professional planting and maintenance

    Our professional planting ensures your garden will thrive over time. We select resilient plants suitable for the sun and wind conditions on your balcony, and provide ongoing maintenance services to keep your urban garden looking perfect.

    Flooring and lighting solutions

    We specialize in installing designed decks, quality flooring, and smart lighting systems that create a magical atmosphere in the evening hours. Our solutions combine weather resistance with elegant design that upgrades the entire space.
